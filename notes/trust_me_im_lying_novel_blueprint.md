# Trust Me, I'm Lying - Novel Blueprint (Psychological Thriller)

## Positioning
- Category: Psychological Thriller / Conspiracy Suspense
- Target length: 75k-82k words
- Structure: Prologue + 24 chapters + optional epilogue

## Core Promise
A group of compulsive liars must tell the truth long enough to expose a financial conspiracy before the conspiracy erases them one by one.

## Act 1 - Setup and Lock-In (Ch 1-6)
1. Empty Chair: Group waits for Dr. Marsh; Emma reveals notebook and cryptic threat.
2. True Lies: Real identities emerge; three links to Harrington Global surface.
3. Family Therapy: Emma reveals she is Marsh's daughter; the group receives Marsh's warning about a sixth member.
4. Verification Night: They split to verify identities and discover each person's story is true in dangerous ways.
5. First Breach: Jonathan's storage locker is hit before he arrives; someone knew location.
6. Lock-In Incident: Attack at Diana's flat confirms active surveillance; group forced into alliance.

## Act 2A - Investigation and Fracture (Ch 7-12)
7. Dead Drop: Marsh's hidden archive recovered from her office wall safe.
8. Mirror Statements: Each gives sworn video testimony in case of death; contradictions appear.
9. Kazmi Holdings: Aisha discovers family shell companies touching MONARCH transfers.
10. The False Witness: Peter's source is found dead; evidence points to staged suicide.
11. Protocol Fragment: Jonathan decrypts partial ledger showing Continuity Protocol expenditures.
12. Midpoint Reversal: Continuity Protocol is not only laundering - it's funding identity continuity operations for elite clients (replacement identities, legal/persona erasure).

## Act 2B - Countermoves and Betrayals (Ch 13-19)
13. Public Leak #1: Emma publishes a controlled teaser; immediate legal and physical blowback.
14. The Handler: Group identifies recurring anonymous facilitator tied to every death.
15. Broken Chain: Internal trust collapses after a leak from inside their secure channel.
16. Casualty: One core ally dies during extraction of offshore key material.
17. Sixth Member: Evidence suggests sixth member has been in contact under assumed identity.
18. Trap House: Group stages false handoff of MONARCH keys to flush watcher.
19. False Resolution: They think they identify the traitor; reveal is a decoy operation.

## Act 3 - Exposure and Endgame (Ch 20-24)
20. Marsh Tapes: Eleanor's final recordings reveal psychological profile of true architect.
21. Court of Public Opinion: Simultaneous release to press, regulators, and international anti-corruption networks.
22. The Real Sixth: Sixth member unmasked from within trusted perimeter.
23. Final Session: Confrontation in therapy-room mirror setting; truth weaponized against manipulator.
24. Burn Notice: Continuity Protocol exposed; surviving members choose whether to keep lying or rebuild truthful identities.

## Character Arc Requirements
- Emma: From observer/performer to accountable truth-teller.
- Jonathan: From self-preservation to whistleblower under own name.
- Aisha: From institutional faith to moral risk against family interests.
- Peter: From performative cynic to credible witness.
- Diana: From detached researcher to emotionally invested protector.

## Bestseller-Grade Craft Constraints
- Every chapter ends with a decision, threat, or irreversible reveal.
- No exposition block longer than ~250 words without interruption/action.
- At least one concrete external pressure event every 2 chapters.
- Each POV scene must change trust status between at least two characters.
- Keep clues auditable: seed early, pay off late, avoid twist-only surprises.
